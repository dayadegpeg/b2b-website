About Us

How We Can Help You Achieve Your Business Goal

Advanced multistreaming platform ( 30+ Social platforms ) with unique capabilities for global content creators & brands worldwide

The vision of degpeg is to empower livestreaming in a way that can be used not only to produce real time content, but build a meaningful business out of it, for content creators or advertisers or associated businesses like : e-commerce, retail, education, gaming etc .

In degpeg, we keep working on our platform to provide amazing livestreaming experience to users of content creators or brands , which can increase engagement, retention rates & boost revenues

Platform key features:

  • Multistreaming.
  • Scheduler.
  • Call to Action.
  • Chat & Live Analytics.
